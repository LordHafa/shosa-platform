How SHOSA SACCO works

A clear path into active membership.

Keep the SACCO journey understandable for first-time users and young alumni coming into the system.

1

Register as alumni

Create your alumni profile first so the system knows who you are and links your participation to the wider SHOSA community.

2

Join or update SACCO

Submit your SACCO membership details so your participation is captured properly before you begin operating as a member.

3

Pay the UGX 50,000 registration fee

The first SACCO payment activates your membership. After that, other SACCO payments and member actions open up normally.
